Privacy Policy

Log X reads your workout data from Apple Health (HealthKit) to display your fitness history, statistics, and summaries. This data is processed entirely on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, health data, or usage analytics to external servers. There are no user accounts, no third-party SDKs, and no tracking of any kind. Photos accessed through the camera or photo library are used solely for creating workout share cards and are never uploaded or stored outside your device. Notifications are delivered locally on your device when a new workout is recorded in Apple Health. If you have any questions, contact us at logxfitness@gmail.com.
