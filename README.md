# Log X

**Your Workout, Visualized.**

Log X is an iOS fitness tracking app that reads your workout data from Apple Health and turns it into beautiful visualizations, statistics, and shareable summaries.

## Features

- **Calendar View** — Interactive calendar showing your workout activity with icons for each workout type
- **Workout Details** — Duration, calories, heart rate graphs, and GPS route maps for each session
- **Monthly Wrapped** — Personalized monthly summaries with stats, charts, and motivational insights — shareable as images
- **Share Cards** — Create custom workout cards with your own background photos
- **50+ Workout Types** — Running, cycling, swimming, strength training, yoga, HIIT, and more
- **Heart Rate Graphs** — Smooth visualizations with min/max BPM
- **Route Maps** — GPS route rendering for outdoor workouts
- **Notifications** — Get notified when new workouts are recorded
- **Dark Mode** — Full light and dark theme support
- **Localization** — English and Korean

## Privacy

Log X processes all data entirely on your device. No data is collected, stored, or transmitted to any server. The app only reads workout data from Apple Health with your permission.

## Tech Stack

- SwiftUI
- HealthKit
- CoreLocation
- PhotosUI

## Requirements

- iOS 18.0+
